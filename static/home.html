Connecting Coasts, Protecting Communities.

AquaSentry ("Ocean Sentry") is an integrated platform empowering citizens, analysts, and authorities to collaboratively report, verify, and respond to ocean hazards in real-time.

Analyst & Authority Portal

How It Works

1. Report

Citizens use the AquaSentry mobile app to instantly report ocean hazards with photos and GPS locations, even from remote areas.

2. Verify

Analysts receive live reports on a central dashboard, verify the information, and update the hazard's status for all teams to see.

3. Oversee

Authorities monitor a high-level dashboard, tracking the status of verified reports to coordinate responses and manage resources effectively.
